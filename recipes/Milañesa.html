Mila単esa

Description

Mila単esa de Pollo is a classic dish that originates from Mexico and is enjoyed with rice and beans.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast
  • 1 Cup bread crumbs
  • 2 Whole Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Olive Oil or Frying Oil
  • 1/2 Tbsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tbsp Pepper

Steps

Before starting, make sure your work area is clean and has all ingredients ready to use

  1. Cut Chicken breast to have a height of less than 1 Inch, pulverize if necessary
  2. Whisk eggs on a shallow or bowl plate
  3. Mix all dry ingredients (Bread Crumbs, Salt, and Pepper) on a shallow plate
  4. Move Chicken Breast into the plate with Whisked Eggs and coat the Chicken Breast in it
  5. Move Chicken Breast from the Egg plate and lay it on the Bread Crumb mix
  6. Coat Chicken Breast in Bread Crumb mix, layering it on multiple times
  7. Place a Pan onto the stove and turn on the heat to Medium
  8. After the Pan has been heated, pour the Oil onto the Pan
  9. When a shimmer becomes apparent on the Oil, place the Chicken Breast onto the Pan
  10. Cook Chicken Breast, 4 Minutes Each Side
  11. Place your Mila単esa on a plate and serve
